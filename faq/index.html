Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

guessbabygender.in is a website that provides fun, informative, and entertaining content related to babies, parenting, and family life. We may also feature curated products through affiliate links to help parents discover useful baby-related items.

No, we do not collect personal information from visitors. However, third-party services like Google AdSense and affiliate programs may collect non-personal information for ads or referral tracking. Please see our Privacy Policy for details.

We use Google AdSense to display ads. These ads help support the website and allow us to continue providing free content. Ads are personalized based on user interests by Google, and you can learn more at Google Ad Policies.

4. What are affiliate links?

Some links on our site are affiliate links. This means we may earn a small commission if you make a purchase through these links, at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products we believe are useful or relevant.

5. Can I submit content or suggestions?

Yes! You can reach us via the Contact Page. While we may not respond to all suggestions individually, we appreciate your ideas and feedback.

6. Is the website suitable for children?

The content is intended for general audiences. Parents should supervise children while browsing the web. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. How can I contact you?

You can reach us via the Contact Page for any questions regarding content, ads, or affiliate links.

8. Can the Privacy Policy or Terms change?

Yes, we may update our Privacy Policy or Terms & Conditions from time to time. We recommend checking these pages periodically for any updates.
